Classic Waffles

Home

Description

These classic waffles are light, crispy, and perfect for breakfast or brunch. Made with a simple batter, they can be topped with your favorite fruits, whipped cream, or syrup for a delightful treat.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil or melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
